# Sales Performance Insights

## Key Findings

### 1. Revenue Distribution
- **West** and **North** regions consistently lead in revenue generation
- Regional multipliers show West at 1.15x and North at 1.1x the baseline
- South region shows lowest revenue at 0.95x baseline

### 2. Seasonal Patterns
- **Q4 (Nov-Dec)** shows the strongest sales with a 1.6x seasonal multiplier
  - Holiday season drives significantly higher visitor counts and order volumes
- **Q1 (Jan-Feb)** is the weakest period at 0.8x baseline
- **Summer (Jun-Jul)** shows a moderate uptick at 1.2x

### 3. Product Performance
- **Laptop Pro** (P001) is the highest revenue product due to high unit price ($1,299.99)
- **Monitor 27in** (P005) and **Tablet 10in** (P009) are the next highest revenue contributors
- **Phone Charger** (P010) and **Wireless Mouse** (P002) have lowest per-order revenue but higher volume

### 4. Conversion Metrics
- Average conversion rates range from 2% to 12% across transactions
- Higher conversion rates tend to correlate with lower-priced products
- Premium products (Laptop, Tablet, Monitor) have lower conversion rates but higher AOV

### 5. Recommendations
1. **Double down on Q4** — Increase marketing spend during Nov-Dec to capitalize on holiday demand
2. **Boost Q1 sales** — Consider promotions or discounts in Jan-Feb to counter the seasonal dip
3. **Regional strategy** — Investigate South region underperformance; consider targeted campaigns
4. **Product bundling** — Bundle high-volume accessories (Mouse, Charger) with premium products
5. **Conversion optimization** — Focus on improving conversion rates for high-AOV products
